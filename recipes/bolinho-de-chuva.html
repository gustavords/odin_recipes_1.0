Bolinho de Chuva Recipe

A deliciously intoxicating ball of deep-fried batter coated with cinnamon-sugar for your enjoyment. A delight of carbohydrates, for yourself and the family to snack on!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 240ml of milk
  • 15ml or a tablespoon of baking powder
  • 240ml of sugar
  • 2 and 1/2 of all-purpose flour
  • 1 litre of frying oil
  • 3 tablespoons of sugar for coating
  • tablespoon of cinnamon for coating

Steps

  1. Mix the eggs, milk baking powder and sugar until the batter mixture is creamy and homogenous.
  2. Heat up a deep enough pan so that the batter may float.
  3. When the oil is around 180Â°C, with a spoon place small portions of the batter and fry until golden on all sides.
  4. Once fried place the cakes onto some paper-towel to remove the excess oil then place the onto the sugar-cinnamon mixture to coat.
