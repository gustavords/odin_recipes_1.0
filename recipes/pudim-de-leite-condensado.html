Pudim de Leite Condensado Recipe

A brazilian flan! A pudding cake of perfect moisture and gelatinous consistency! A perfect desert for any season!

Ingredients

    Pudding

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 3 eggs
  • milk (same amount as the can of condensed milk, 1:1)

    • Syrup

  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup or 120ml of water

Steps

  1. Pudding:
    1. Mix the eggs well in the mixer.
    2. Add the milk and condensed-milk to the mixed eggs.
  2. Syrup:
    1. Heat up the sugar in a pan until brown. Add the water and let it thicken.
  3. Get a medium angel cake mold pan and place your pudding batter. Bake in the oven for ~45mins at 220Â°C inside a larger ban with boiling water.
  4. Stick a fork to see if its cooked all the way through.
  5. Wait until fully cooled, then remove from pan onto plate. Pour syrup on top of pudding.
